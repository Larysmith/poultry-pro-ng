Questions
Frequently asked
Does it really work without internet? +
Yes. Once you open the app the first time with internet, it installs on your phone and works completely offline after that. Your data saves on your phone, not on a server. Nigerian network problems will not affect your farm records.
Do I need to download it from the App Store? +
No. Just open the link in Chrome (Android) or Safari (iPhone). Chrome will ask if you want to "Add to Home Screen" — tap yes and it becomes an icon on your phone like a regular app. No App Store, no download, no Google Play.
What happens to my data if I switch phones? +
Your data is stored on your current phone. Before switching, tap the Settings gear icon ⚙ in the app and download a backup file. When you set up the new phone, open the app and restore from that backup. All your farm records will be back in 30 seconds.

Think of it this way: one bag of broiler finisher costs ₦19,000. If tracking your FCR helps you catch poor feed efficiency one week early and save even half a bag per batch — the Pro subscription has paid for itself. Most Pro farmers find they save far more than that.
